Frequently Asked Questions

What is the setup process like with Easy Tee?

We offer complimentary setup for all new customers. Just send us a few details about your schedule and pricing and we'll configure everything for you. The first time you log in, just connect your Square account and you can start booking tee times. We can have most customers fully set up in less than 24 hours.

Can I import my customers from another tee sheet product?

During onboarding you sync all of your customers from Square to Easy Tee, so all of your existing customers will automatically be imported. The sync process also syncs customer groups, so your members, juniors, and all other groups will sync as well.

We don't use Square for our POS. Can we still use Easy Tee Golf?

Yes, you can still use Easy Tee Golf, but we highly recommend leveraging the full Square ecosystem to unlock the ultimate operational efficiency and control. We chose to partner with Square because we believe it is the best POS system for a golf course. In addition to processing payments, Square provides tools that help you run an efficient and modern operation.

Please note, if you want to process payments through Easy Tee, you will need a free Square account, as Square is our exclusive payment processor. Payments processed through Square will be subject to Square's standard, flat-rate payment processing fees.

Does Easy Tee work for golf simulators?

Yes! Easy Tee for Simulators has everything you need to manage your schedule, payments, and online booking. It includes all of our flexible scheduling and pricing options and integrates with Square POS. Easy Tee makes running your golf simulator business easy!
